About Cphino

Cphino is a deep-technology startup founded by alumni of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) and Delhi Technological University (DTU), driven by a shared vision to redefine how chemical engineering processes are designed, optimized, and scaled using Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

At Cphino, we bridge the gap between traditional chemical engineering wisdom and modern data-driven intelligence. Our mission is to bring intelligence, efficiency, sustainability, and predictability to chemical processes across industrial and non-industrial domains by embedding AI at the core of decision-making.

The Significance of Our Idea

Chemical processes lie at the heart of industries such as pharmaceuticals, energy, materials, agriculture, food processing, polymers, specialty chemicals, semiconductors, and environmental engineering.

  • Limited real-time intelligence
  • High energy and resource wastage
  • Long development and scale-up cycles
  • Reactive quality control instead of predictive assurance
  • Heavy dependence on trial-and-error experimentation

Cphino’s core idea is simple yet transformative: What if chemical processes could learn, predict, adapt, and optimize themselves?

What We Do

Process design and simulation
Process optimization and control
Fault detection and root-cause analysis
Quality prediction and assurance
Energy and resource efficiency optimization
Scale-up and technology transfer
Sustainability and emissions reduction
R&D acceleration and experimentation intelligence

How Cphino Impacts the Industry

Predictive Operations: Anticipating failures and deviations before they occur.

Faster Innovation Cycles: Shortening R&D timelines using intelligent learning systems.

Intelligent Optimization: Continuous optimization for yield, cost, and sustainability.

Democratizing Intelligence: Making advanced chemical AI accessible to startups, MSMEs, and research labs.

Why This Matters Now

The chemical industry faces rising energy costs, stricter regulations, increasing complexity, and a shortage of skilled experts. Artificial Intelligence is no longer optional — it is essential.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in AI-driven chemical engineering, enabling smarter, safer, and more sustainable chemical processes.

Our Philosophy

  • AI should augment human expertise, not replace it
  • Engineering solutions must be interpretable and reliable
  • Innovation happens at the intersection of domain knowledge and intelligence
  • Technology should serve industry, society, and sustainability together

Cphino is not just building AI tools.

We are shaping the future of chemical engineering.
